Before this backpack, I had two other cat carriers: a hard-framed duffle
bag and a backpack. When I have just one cat to carry, I prefer the duffle
bag. It is easier to pick up and put down, as most of the time I'm only
carrying my cat to and from the car. The backpack was mostly useful if I
needed to carry two cats at once or over long distances.

With this purchase, I now own two cat-carrying backpacks. Between the two,
the PetTemp backpack is far superior. Unlike either my previous backpack or
even the framed duffle bag, the PetTemp folds nicely and easily for
storage, while still having a reasonably strong frame when fully assembled.

It also has a nice, solid back, which makes it much easier to carry than my previous backpack that only had a wireframe. I'm over 6'2", so it is a little small for me. Like the previous backpack, it is awkward to carry by hand; you are going to want to have it on your back or the floor.

I set up the expansion compartment and the cats went in to explore. Well cat really, it was too crapped for both of them at the same time. It added an additional 70% space to the inside of the carrier, which is a great feature if you're not going to be moving around but still need to keep the cat safely inside.

**Pros:**
* Easily foldable for storage
* Nice back support
* Expansion compartment helps increase its space when you have the time to set it up


**Cons:**
* Not weighted very well for being carried in a single hand

Overall, I can easily recommend it if you are in the market for a cat carrier backpack.
