Today [[Summer Elsawi]], [[Mirium Elsawi]], [[Kasey Scoboria]], and [[Naoko Huffaker]] climbed the main mountain on [[Miyajima]]. Wake up early in the morning and towards [[Miyajima]]. 
![[IMG_7543.jpg]]

Kasey convined us that we should take the tram up and climbing down.  This was the right The ladies, especially [[Kasey Scoboria]] didn't have much engery left afterwards. The desire to avoid stairs held a shadow over the rest of the trip. The tram ride itself was lovely. With a view above the tree tops looking down across the steep valley walls. 
![[2023-miyajima-tram-niecies.jpg]]

There is still a good 30 minute steep walk from the tam, past the shrine, and then onto the temple. 
![[2023-miyajima-shire-nieces.jpg]]
At the top of the peak there is a observation tower with three levels.  The first level has the visitor center and toilet.  The middle level has a series of raised wooden, no shoes please, platforms ringed open windows. We spread out the food we had purchased at the combini just before we got onto the ferry on the second level and had outselves a feast. 
![[miyajima-top-feast.jpg]]

The view was also spectacular.
![[2023-miyamija-view-nieces.jpg]]

While not as hard as the hike up, the hike down is hard.
![[2023-miyajima-stairs-nieces.jpg]]
The hike includes some love little shrines.
![[2023-miyamija-small-shrine-niecies.jpg]]

Then we step through, in the wrong direction the Nimon (仁王門)。
![[2034-miyajima-nimon-gate-nieces 1.jpg]]

After we came down, which all agreed was a delight, we found out self west of the main temple.  Two boys and a girl asked to have thier photo taken with the ladies.  I understood right away, but it took a bit of explaining before the ladies understood.  In the end they agreed and if it doesn't repeat, it was a good story. Sadly we failed to capture our own photo of the event. So will have to live only in our memories. 

We come out at the wrong gate, so had to walk our way around to the other side. Given the state of the ladies legs by then, it was a bit of a stroll. We had to trade off light angle with water depth, higher tide lower light, but I think we got a better deal.
